Kizza's Lamingtons

Lamingtons are a classic Australian dessert that consist of a buttery sponge cake coated in chocolate and coconut.

The sponge in this recipe is moist and buttery, and can be optionally sandwiched with jam and cream

Ingredients

  • 125g unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs, room temperature
  • 1 3/4 cups plain flour
  • 3 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 4 cups icing sugar
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 150ml boiling water
  • 4 cups shredded coconut
  • Strawberry jam (optional)
  • 1 cup cream (optional)

Ingredients

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and line a 20cm (8 inch) square cake tin
  2. In a large bowl, cream the butter, sugar and vanilla extract together until light and fluffy
  3. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition
  4. Sift the flour and baking powder together and add to the butter mixture, alternating with the milk. Mix until just combined
  5. Pour the mixture into the prepared tin and bake for 30-35 minutes or until a skewer inserted into the centre comes out clean
  6. Allow the cake to cool in the tin for 5 minutes before turning out onto a wire rack to cool completely
  7. Cut the cake into 15 squares
  8. To make the icing, combine the icing sugar, cocoa powder, butter and boiling water in a large bowl. Stir until smooth
  9. Place the coconut in a shallow dish
  10. Using two forks, dip each square of cake into the icing, then roll in the coconut to coat
  11. Place the lamingtons on a wire rack to set
  12. If desired, spread a little strawberry jam and whipped cream between two lamingtons to make a sandwich. Dust with icing sugar and serve
